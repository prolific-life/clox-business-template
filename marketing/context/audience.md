## Business model

- **Type:** _b2b | b2c | b2b2c (from plan §6)_
- **Motion:** _sales-led | product-led | content-led_

## Segments

For each segment (2–3, no demographic mush):

### <segment name>
- **Who:** role / life situation
- **Pain:** the problem in their words
- **Trigger:** the moment they go looking for a solution
- **Watering holes:** where they actually are (subreddits, LinkedIn
  circles, newsletters, search terms)
- **Objection:** the #1 reason they don't buy
- **Proof that lands:** what convinces them (case study, demo, price)
