# Deck outline — <deck name>

> Working narrative for `pitch/decks/<slug>/`. The argument lives
> here; slides.json is its published form. Standard fundraising arc
> below — cut/merge slides freely, never pad.

1. **Title** — name, one-liner (brand positioning line), logo.
2. **Problem** — the pain in the customer's words (from
   `marketing/context/audience.md`); why now.
3. **Solution** — what we built; the wedge vs alternatives
   (`marketing/context/competitors.md`).
4. **Product** — 1–2 screenshots of the REAL app; one flow, not a
   feature list.
5. **Market** — bottom-up sizing from the ICP segments; sourced.
6. **Traction** — real numbers from `metrics/` (KPIs, growth,
   engagement). If pre-traction: the validated signal so far.
7. **Business model** — how we charge (from
   `marketing/context/offers.md` + plan).
8. **Go to market** — the motion that's working (from the GTM plan +
   campaign results — cite `marketing/campaigns/*/results.md`).
9. **Competition** — honest map; our wedge per alternative.
10. **Team / story** — from `brand/story/`; why us.
11. **The ask** — amount, use of funds, the milestone it buys.

Per slide note: claim → evidence → so-what. Speaker notes carry the
narration (voice from `brand/voice/`); the slide carries the punch.
